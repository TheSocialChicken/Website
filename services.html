What We Offer

Three products.
One philosophy.

Everything we do is built around the same idea: experience first, theory second. Play before you commit. Test before you decide. Learn by doing.

01 / Agile Working Training

Learn agile
by playing it first

Most agile adoptions fail before they start. Not because agile is wrong, but because you can't get buy-in from a presentation. We start with games that make people feel agile, then guide them into real Scrum and Kanban adoption.

Agile Kickstart

A half-day session of agile games that creates genuine team buy-in. Perfect as the first step before any agile program.

Scrum Adoption Program

A structured 4 to 8 week program that takes your team from games to real working Scrum boards and sprint rhythms.

Kanban for Teams

Visual workflow management that actually gets used. We implement Kanban in a way that fits how your team already thinks.

"Buy-in is not a checkbox. It is something people feel after they have experienced agile in a game. Everything else follows much more naturally from there."

Christiaan Verhoef, Founder

02 / Innovation Consulting

Solve problems
that keep coming back

Some problems don't go away because the solution was wrong. They go away and come back because the root cause was never found. We help organisations dig to the real issue and design solutions that hold, including where automation can genuinely help.

Problem Sprint

A focused 3-day deep dive that maps your problem space, identifies root causes, and produces a clear action plan.

Automation Audit

We identify where automation genuinely helps your operation and where it would just add complexity. Honest, practical, specific.

Innovation Workshop

A facilitated session using the Triple Layer Business Model Canvas and game-theory-based ideation to surface your best ideas and stress-test them.

03 / Simulations & Games

Play your way to
better decisions

Serious games are the fastest way to teach complex systems. We design and run games that reveal how your organisation really works, built on game theory, the conscious competence model, and blended learning principles.

Classic Serious Games

The Beer Game, supply chain simulations, and other proven formats that teach systems thinking in hours, not weeks.

Digital Simulations

Custom Game Design

We design entirely new serious games around your specific system, challenge, or learning objective. Your context, your game.

Common Questions

Frequently asked

What types of organisations do you work with? +

We work with educational institutions, startups, scale-ups, SMEs, and public sector organisations. Anyone who is ready to learn by doing and decide by simulating is a good fit.

What is the difference between Agile Training and a Simulation? +

Agile Working Training is a program that changes how a team works day to day, using games and real Scrum or Kanban adoption. Simulations and Games are focused experiences for teaching systems thinking or testing decisions. Many of our engagements combine both.

How long does a typical engagement take? +

A single game session can run half a day. An Agile adoption program typically runs 4 to 8 weeks. Innovation consulting ranges from a focused 3-day sprint to a 12-week project. We scope every engagement in a free discovery call before any commitment.

Do you work internationally? +

Yes. We are based in the Netherlands and deliver in-person, virtual, and blended formats globally.

Can I combine products? +

Absolutely, and it often makes sense. A common combination is an Innovation Consulting sprint followed by a custom serious game that stress-tests the solution with your team. We can design whatever combination fits your context.
