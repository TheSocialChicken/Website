Terms of Service

Last updated: April 2026. Governed by Dutch law.

1. About these terms

These Terms of Service apply to all consulting, training, and simulation engagements delivered by SocialChicken (Christiaan Verhoef, Netherlands) to clients worldwide. By entering into an engagement with SocialChicken, you agree to these terms.

These terms apply to all service formats: in-person, virtual, and blended delivery.

2. Services

SocialChicken provides three categories of service:

  • ●Agile Working Training: facilitated programs using serious games, Scrum adoption, and Kanban implementation.
  • ●Innovation Consulting: structured problem identification, root cause analysis, automation audits, and solution design.
  • ●Simulations and Games: facilitated serious games, digital simulation environments, and custom game design.

The exact scope, format, deliverables, and pricing for each engagement are agreed in a written proposal before work begins. The proposal takes precedence over these general terms where they conflict.

3. Engagement process

Every engagement begins with a free discovery call. No work is commenced and no fees are due until a written proposal has been agreed by both parties. Agreement may be communicated by email.

SocialChicken reserves the right to decline engagements that are not a suitable fit.

4. Payment terms

Unless agreed otherwise in the proposal:

  • ●A 50% deposit is invoiced upon agreement and is due before work begins.
  • ●The remaining 50% is invoiced upon delivery and is due within 30 days of the invoice date.
  • ●Multi-week engagements may be invoiced in milestones as agreed in the proposal.
  • ●Travel costs for in-person delivery outside the Netherlands are quoted separately and invoiced at cost.

Invoices are issued in EUR unless USD is agreed in writing. Applicable Dutch VAT (BTW) will be added where legally required.

5. Cancellation and rescheduling

For single-session engagements (e.g. Agile Kickstart, game session):

  • ●Cancellation or rescheduling with more than 7 days notice: no charge, deposit transferred to new date.
  • ●Cancellation with 3 to 7 days notice: 50% of the session fee is charged to cover preparation costs.
  • ●Cancellation with less than 3 days notice: 100% of the session fee is charged.

For multi-week programs, cancellation terms are agreed in the proposal. SocialChicken will provide reasonable notice if unforeseen circumstances prevent delivery and will offer a rescheduled date or a proportional refund.

You may not resell, license, or train others in SocialChicken's proprietary methodologies without written permission.

SocialChicken treats all client information shared during an engagement as confidential. We do not disclose client identities, strategies, or outcomes to third parties without written consent.
