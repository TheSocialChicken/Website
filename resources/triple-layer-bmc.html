Triple Layer
Business Model Canvas

The Triple Layer Business Model Canvas extends the classic Business Model Canvas by Alex Osterwalder with two additional layers: an environmental layer and a social layer. The result is a tool that maps your business model across all three dimensions of sustainable value creation.

What is the Triple Layer BMC?

Layer 1: Economic

The Classic Business Model Canvas

The original 9-block canvas that maps your value proposition, customer segments, channels, revenue streams, key resources, key activities, key partners, and cost structure. The foundation that everything else builds on.

Layer 2: Environmental

The Environmental Layer

Maps the environmental flows and impacts of your business model. What natural resources does your model consume? What does it return? Where are the environmental costs and what is your ecological value proposition?

Layer 3: Social

The Social Layer

Maps the social value your model creates and the stakeholders it touches beyond customers and partners. Who benefits? Who is affected? What is your social value proposition and what social costs does your model create?
