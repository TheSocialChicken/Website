Agile Working Training

Learn agile by
playing it first.

The fastest way to kill agile adoption is to start with a slide deck. We start with a game. Once people feel what agile is, the Scrum boards and sprint rituals make sense by themselves.

Discuss a Training Program

Why We Start with Games

Buy-in is not a checkbox.

Most agile transformations fail because the people doing the work were never convinced it was worth doing. You can't convince people with a framework document. You have to let them experience the problem themselves.

We use agile games to create that experience. In a single session, your team feels the chaos of uncoordinated work, the power of a shared backlog, and the relief of a clear sprint goal. Everything that follows is just reinforcing what they already discovered.

The Learning Journey

From clueless to capable

01

Unconscious Incompetence

Your team does not know what they do not know. The agile game creates the moment of realisation: "This is harder than I thought, and we can do it better."

02

Conscious Incompetence

Now they know they have a gap. This is where motivation lives. We introduce Scrum and Kanban as tools to fill the gap they just felt, not as compliance requirements.

03

Conscious Competence

Your team runs their first real sprints with support. They are thinking about what they are doing. We are there to coach, unblock, and help them build the muscle memory.

04

Unconscious Competence

Agile is just how your team works now. The boards, the rituals, the mindset. No one has to remind them. That is when we know the program worked.

What We Offer

Training programs

Program 01

Agile Kickstart

A half-day session of agile games that creates real buy-in. Perfect as the first step before any agile program or transformation. Works as a standalone event too.

Program 02

Scrum Team Program

A 4 to 8 week program that takes your team from games to real working Scrum. Includes sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives. Real boards, real work.

Program 03

Kanban for Teams

Visual workflow management that actually gets used. We implement Kanban boards that fit how your team already thinks and gradually introduce WIP limits and flow metrics.

Program 04

Agile Leadership

For managers and team leads. How to lead in an agile environment, how to stop managing and start coaching, and how to create the conditions for self-organising teams.

Program 05

Custom Programs

Built from scratch around your specific context. Your team, your challenges, your desired outcomes. We design the games, the program arc, and the adoption support.

Delivery Formats

We come to you

In-Person

Virtual

Fully remote delivery using digital game platforms and collaborative tools. Works for distributed and global teams.

Blended

A combination of asynchronous preparation, live game sessions, and ongoing coaching during real sprint cycles.
