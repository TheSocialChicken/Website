Questions About Pricing

Good to know

Is there a minimum commitment? +

No minimum commitment. Every engagement starts with a free discovery call. If we are a good fit, we scope the work together before anything is signed.

Can I combine products? +

Yes. Combined engagements are common and often make sense. For example, a Game session followed by an Agile adoption program. We will build a combined package that fits your goals and budget.

Do you offer educational institution discounts? +

Yes. Universities, colleges, and non-profit educational organisations are eligible for discounted rates. Mention this in your discovery call and we will adjust the proposal accordingly.

Are travel costs included? +

Travel costs for in-person delivery outside the Netherlands are quoted separately and transparently before any commitment. Virtual delivery has no additional costs.

Still not sure which tier fits?

Book a free 30-minute discovery call. We will help you figure out exactly what you need before you spend anything.
